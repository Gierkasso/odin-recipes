Description

There are some studies in which are shown that a single glass of honeydew melon juice has 34% of the bodyâ€™s Vitamin C requirement. We know that the Vitamin C can strengthen and boost the immune system

Ingredients

  • 1 spoon of Honey
  • 1 melon
  • 800ml of water
  • 3 leaves of mint

Steps

  1. Cut the melon in order to remove the skin of it, and cut it in pieces
  2. Wash the mint leaves with water
  3. Put all the ingredients in a mixer
  4. Mix it up, and enjoy!
