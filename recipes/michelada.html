Ou Michelada

Description

The most lazy way to make chilean michelada, perfect for refreshing your hot days

The regular way to do it demands no such more efforts, but this is the fastest way and the final result is very balance for every kiss(drink) to the glass

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 beer of your preference

Steps

  1. Squeze the lemon in to the glass
  2. Add the salt and mix strongly
  3. Add only little bit of beer, to make it grow, and mix
  4. Add the rest of the mix and mix smoothly
  5. Enjoy like being in the beach !
